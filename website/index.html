ABOUT

Why QubitCoin?

Quantum computers threaten the elliptic curve cryptography securing every Bitcoin transaction. QubitCoin is a Bitcoin fork that replaces ECDSA with NIST-standardized post-quantum signatures — today.

Quantum Resistant

ML-DSA-65 (FIPS 204) lattice-based signatures resist both classical and quantum attacks. Your coins stay safe even when large-scale quantum computers arrive.

Seamless Migration

BTC holders prove ownership with a one-time ECDSA signature to claim their full aggregated address balance as quantum-safe QTC. No trust, no intermediaries.

Bitcoin DNA

Same UTXO model. Same SHA-256 proof-of-work. Same decentralized ethos. QubitCoin preserves what makes Bitcoin great — and future-proofs it.

Under the Hood

FIPS 204

ML-DSA-65 (Dilithium)

Module-Lattice Digital Signature Algorithm at security level 3. Based on the hardness of the Module Learning With Errors problem — no known quantum algorithm can efficiently solve it.

PROCESS

How It Works

Migrate from Bitcoin to QubitCoin in three steps.

Snapshot Bitcoin

A Bitcoin full node dumps the complete UTXO set via dumptxoutset. ~160M UTXOs are parsed, filtered to P2PKH + P2WPKH, and aggregated into ~50M address balances. The genesis block commits to a merkle root of this snapshot.

Claim Your Balance

Sign a claim message with your Bitcoin private key. The network verifies your HASH160 matches a snapshot address and binds your full aggregated balance to a new ML-DSA-65 public key. One claim per address.

Transact Quantum-Safe

Once claimed, your QTC coins live in quantum-resistant UTXOs. All future transactions use ML-DSA-65 signatures — safe from both classical and quantum adversaries.
